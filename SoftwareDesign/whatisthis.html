Cell Simulator
By: Andres Galindo

Start simulator!

Th simulator takes you into a not so distant future where nano bots are the most effective form of cancer treatment. In this environment cells must seek food in order to survive and pass on their genetic code. However the nanobots are on constant surveilance and will destroy any cell that their scanners pick up as cancerous.

Legend

The cells only purpose in life is to eat and mate. It seeks for a mate once its nice and full but if it fails to consume enough food, it will die of starvation. Every cell has its DNA code and upon mating it passes on half of his genetic code and half of its partners code. By clicking on the cell you can see its current mate aswell as its DNA strand.
